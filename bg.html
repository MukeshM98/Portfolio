Medicinal AI Chatbot

This project is a conversational AI designed to provide basic, preliminary medicinal advice. It leverages the LangChain framework for structuring the language model interactions and is powered by the fast Groq LPU inference engine. Users can ask health-related questions in natural language, and the chatbot provides information based on its training data. It's intended as an informational tool, not a substitute for professional medical advice.
